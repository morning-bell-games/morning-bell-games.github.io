FOOTY CARDS


Overview

Aussie Rules Football is a beautiful game. More than a game, if you will.

This sport has birthed unique cultures, unbreakable relationships, unforgettable moments, and the most gorgeous mullets you’ll ever see.

The aim of Footy Cards is to capture even a crumb of this beauty and relay it in a fast-paced, tactical computer card game made by the footy nerd, for the footy nerd.

Gameplay

Each player has a deck of 18 cards. Throughout 4 quarters, players will use these cards to score points, activate unique effects, and win additional zone effects across the 3 zones (Defence, Midfield, and Forward).

Cards use their Momentum to win zone effects and their Score Power to score (or defend) points.

The 3 zone effects will change each quarter, meaning each quarter will play differently and require a different set of tactics.

Which zones do you favour? How can you use your card effect synergies to kick a bag? What is your opponent planning? Should you favour scoring big or nullifying your opponents strategy?

And all the while, you're under a short time restriction.

Footy Cards is about making quick tactical choices and thinking one step ahead of your opponent.
